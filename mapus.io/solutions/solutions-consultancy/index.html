ELEVATE YOUR DIGITAL CONSULTANCY SERVICES

MapUs gives consultants the tools to initiate successful digital transformation. Boost your digital profile and give new life to your own services.

CONSULTING FIRMS USE MAPUS FOR...

Roadmapping

Use the roadmap embedded in the MapUs competence model to develop digital transformation strategies for your clients.

Visualisation

All the data generated in MapUs is visualised in real-time, compelling animations, allowing you to present and convey results in a dynamic way. 

Service integration

Connect your own internal resources and services to the MapUs platform to qualify and elevate your digital consultancy.

GAIN ORGANIZATIONAL OVERVIEW

MapUs makes initiating digital transformation easy by generating a complete and comprehensive organizational overview in just one simple step. Have your clients complete the digital competence survey, and you’ll have all the information you need to start working purposefully with digital development.

GENERATE IMPACTFUL REPORTS

We know that well-presented data plays a huge role in digital consultancy. With MapUs’ versatile report generation, you can create visually engaging baseline and benchmark reports for every project. Dive deep into data on specific competences or groups and hone in on how you best help your clients create meaningful progress.

INTEGRATE YOUR OWN SERVICES EFFORTLESSLY

After getting a solid overview of your clients’ needs, MapUs can help to display your services as the natural next step in your clients’ development. Simply connect your own resources such as courses, workshops, conferences etc. to the survey and make the transition from your initial consultancy to your broader service catalogue feel seamless.

CUSTOMIZE TO FIT YOUR EXACT NEEDS

The flexibility incorporated in the MapUs platform allows for endless possibilities for customization. No matter the task, we can help you create the framework that best enables you to excel in advising your clients with your digital consultancy.

Generate Leads and awareness

Having a tool like MapUs to showcase on your online channels is a great asset when it comes to lead generation. With its strong visual DNA and open interface, people want to engage. And when they do, you can lead them through to your broader service catalogue and collect permissions from potential future clients.

Cases: Impactful digital concultancy

Enabling versatile digital consultancy
Komponent were looking for an accessible tool to elevate their consultancy on digitalization to Danish municipalities. In order to make their services relevant to the entire municipal organization, it had to be versatile and customizable, and this is why they choose MapUs. It enabled them to map employees, hold workshops and kickstart the digital dialogue.
